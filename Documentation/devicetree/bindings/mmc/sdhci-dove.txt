* Marvell sdhci-dove controller

Required properties:
- compatible: Should be "marvell,dove-sdhci".
- reg: Physical base address of the sdhci controller.
- interrupts: interrupt nr of the sdhci controller.

Example:

sdio0: sdio@92000 {
	compatible = "marvell,dove-sdhci";
	reg = <0x92000 0x100>;
	interrupts = <35>;
};
